# Assignment: IT in Different Industries

**Type:** Individual Assignment  
**Deliverable:** Word Document

---

## Description

For this assignment, I chose three companies from different industries and explored how the integration of information technology has advanced each company's operations, profits, and organizational structure. For each company I analyzed technology integration, operational advancements, profit impact, and organizational improvement.

**Companies Selected:**
- **Amazon** — Retail / E-Commerce & Logistics
- **Allegheny Health Network (AHN)** — Healthcare
- **Tesla** — Automotive / Manufacturing

I chose AHN as one of my companies because it is my employer, which gave me a personal connection to the material and a real-world perspective on how technology has changed healthcare operations.

---

## Key Findings

**Amazon** has deployed hundreds of thousands of robotic units across its fulfillment centers, cutting fulfillment costs by up to 25% and reducing order cycle times significantly through AI-driven automation.

**Allegheny Health Network** implemented the Epic EMR system across all 14 hospitals and partnered with AI company Abridge to deploy ambient clinical documentation technology — eliminating over $57 million in supply chain discrepancies and reducing clinician burnout.

**Tesla** controls approximately 80% of its supply chain through vertical integration and delivers vehicle improvements via over-the-air software updates, eliminating the need for physical recalls and allowing continuous product improvement post-sale.
