**Module:** L04  
**Points:** 10  
**Type:** Individual Assignment

## Description

For this lab I virtually dissected two devices using the iFixit teardown website. I identified and labeled eight core hardware components in each device including the CPU, RAM, power supply, motherboard, hard drive, network adapter, and input/output devices. The findings were compiled into a PowerPoint presentation with screenshots and labeled diagrams.

## Reflection

This was honestly one of my favorite assignments of the semester because it tapped directly into something I genuinely love. Taking apart computers and understanding what makes them tick has been a passion of mine since I was a kid. Doing it virtually through iFixit still scratched that itch and gave me a more structured way of thinking about the components I already knew intuitively. Labeling and explaining each part in presentation form also forced me to articulate things I knew by feel rather than by name, which was a useful exercise.
