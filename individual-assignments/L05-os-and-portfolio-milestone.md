## Assignment 1: Choose the Best OS

### Description

For this assignment I researched and compared different operating systems to determine which would be the best fit for a given scenario. The assignment required evaluating options based on factors like compatibility, cost, security, and ease of use, and making a justified recommendation.

### Reflection

Comparing operating systems made me think more critically about something I had always taken for granted. I had always just used Windows without questioning whether it was the best tool for the job. Researching the strengths and weaknesses of different OS options gave me a much clearer picture of why certain systems are favored in certain environments, which is directly relevant to cybersecurity where understanding the OS landscape matters a lot.

## Assignment 2: Learning Portfolio Milestone 1

### Description

For this assignment I selected GitHub as my portfolio platform and created a detailed outline for how my final portfolio would be organized. I evaluated different platform options and made the case for GitHub based on its direct relevance to the IT and cybersecurity fields, its repository-based structure for organizing work, and my prior exposure to it through coursework.

### Reflection

This assignment pushed me to think ahead about how I wanted to present my work before I had actually done most of it, which was harder than it sounds. Looking back, I wish I had stayed more on top of updating the portfolio as the semester went on rather than letting it pile up at the end. That said, the outline I created gave me a solid roadmap that made pulling everything together much more manageable. Choosing GitHub also ended up being the right call since it is a platform I will realistically use throughout my career in IT.
