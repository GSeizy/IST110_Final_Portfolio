# L11: What Do Companies Know About You?

**Module:** L11  
**Points:** 10  
**Type:** Individual Assignment

---

## Description

For this assignment I explored how much personal information is publicly available about me online. I reviewed the Data and Privacy section of my Google account to see what information had been collected, Googled my own name to see what came up, and explored two data aggregator websites to see what personal details they had on file. I answered questions about what I found and whether there was a way to have entries removed.

## Reflection

This assignment was genuinely eye-opening in a way I did not fully expect. I knew that data was being collected about me but seeing it laid out across multiple platforms made the scale of it much more concrete. The data aggregator sites in particular were unsettling since they pull together information from multiple public sources into a single profile without you ever having directly given them anything. It connected directly to the privacy paradox discussion from earlier in the course and made abstract privacy concerns feel very personal and immediate.
