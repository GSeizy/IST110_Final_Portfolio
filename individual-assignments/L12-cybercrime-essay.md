# L12: Cybercrime Essay

**Module:** L12  
**Points:** 10  
**Type:** Individual Assignment

---

## Description

For this assignment I wrote a short argumentative essay on a cybercrime and punishment debate topic. I selected a position, researched arguments and evidence from both sides, presented a clear argument for my chosen side, addressed counterarguments from the opposing view, and concluded with a summary of my main points. At least one credible source was cited in APA format. The paper was a maximum of two double-spaced pages.

## Reflection

Writing an argumentative essay on cybercrime felt relevant in a way that a lot of assignments do not. Cybercrime is not an abstract concept, it affects real people and organizations every day, and the questions around how to punish it are genuinely complicated. Researching both sides of the debate before committing to a position forced me to engage seriously with arguments I did not initially agree with, which is a skill that matters well beyond this class. It also gave me early exposure to the legal and regulatory side of cybersecurity, which I know comes up more formally in later courses.
