# Individual Assignment: IT in Different Industries

**Module:** L12  
**Points:** 10  
**Type:** Individual Assignment

---

## Description

For this assignment I chose three companies from different industries and explored how the integration of information technology has advanced each company's operations, profits, and organization. The three companies I selected were Amazon (Retail/Logistics), Allegheny Health Network (Healthcare), and Tesla (Automotive/Manufacturing).

I chose AHN specifically because it is my employer. Seeing how deeply technology has changed the healthcare supply chain and clinical documentation at a network I work within every day made the assignment feel a lot more real and personal than just picking random companies.

## Reflection

This assignment gave me a new appreciation for how much technology is quietly running in the background of industries I interact with every day. I knew Amazon was tech-driven but the scale of their robotics and AI operations was still impressive to research. The AHN section hit closest to home since I work within that network and see some of those supply chain systems firsthand. What stood out most was how across three completely different industries, the common thread was always the same: technology reduced costs, improved accuracy, and changed how people do their jobs on a daily basis.
