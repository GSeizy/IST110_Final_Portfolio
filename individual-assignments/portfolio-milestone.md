**Module:** L05  
**Points:** 100  
**Type:** Individual Assignment

## Description

For this assignment, I selected GitHub as my portfolio platform and created a detailed outline for how my final portfolio would be organized. I evaluated different platform options and made the case for GitHub based on its direct relevance to the cybersecurity and IT fields, its repository-based structure for organizing work, and my prior exposure to it through coursework.

## Reflection

This assignment pushed me to think ahead about how I wanted to present my work before I had actually done most of it, which was harder than it sounds. Looking back, I wish I had stayed more on top of updating the portfolio as the semester went on rather than letting it pile up at the end. That said, the outline I created gave me a solid roadmap that made pulling everything together at the end much more manageable. Choosing GitHub also ended up being the right call since it is a platform I will realistically use throughout my career in IT.
